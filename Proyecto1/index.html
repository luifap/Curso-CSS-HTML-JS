The most profitable businesses in Colombia

High competition, low wages, age range, English proficiency and little work experience are part of what people consider to be the main reasons for not getting a job in one of the country's companies, revealed a study by Adecco, the Colombian multinational human resources.

Pet store:

Pets are another member of the family, therefore, it needs services similar to that of people. Hairdressing, veterinary, sale of accessories and even a nursery can be a good alternative.

Cleaning service:

Cleaning offices, apartments and houses are in great demand. Lack of time due to overwork is one of the main causes.

Sale and shoe repair:

Shoes are as important as clothing and will never go out of style. That is why it is important to be creative if you are going to offer footwear, which are different styles and which have been little seen in the market. Shoe repair shops are also a good option.

Coffee shops:

Although it is a very competitive business, it is profitable. It is recommended to buy the cafeteria already established. If you decide to start from scratch, note that there is not much competition in the place.
